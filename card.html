Sloth

The sloth is so named because of its very low metabolism and deliberate movements, sloth being related to the word slow.

  • Scientific name: Bradypus varaiegatus
  • Activity: 9.6 h/day
  • Favourite food: cecropia
  • Averange length: 60-80 cm

Sloths are arboreal mammals noted for slowness of movement and for spending most of their lives hanging upside down in the trees of the tropical rainforests of South America and Central America.
